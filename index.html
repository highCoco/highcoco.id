About High Coco

Indonesian craftsmanship, built for global export standards

High Coco was founded in 2020 with a single purpose: turning Indonesia's abundant coconut shell waste into a clean-burning, export-grade energy product. From our factory floor to the final container seal, every batch is produced under consistent process control so buyers in the Middle East, Europe and beyond receive the same quality, shipment after shipment.

    Production Process

    From raw coconut shell to sealed export container

    Scroll to watch each stage light up — the same nine steps every batch goes through before it reaches your port.

    01
    Raw Material

    Selected coconut shells received & sorted.

    02
    Carbonization

    Shells carbonized into raw charcoal.

    03
    Crushing

    Charcoal crushed into fine powder.

    04
    Mixing

    Powder blended with natural binder.

    05
    Hydraulic Press

    06
    Drying
